What we are Looking For

  • Critical infrastructure and public utilities

    Transport, water, electricity and associated organisations, as well as national research facilities, for example the Opal nuclear research reactor, where Cyber Security is an increasing risk.

  • Healthcare

    Encompassing health data management, as well as security for emerging on-line, real-time treatment over distance (i.e. remote telemedicine).

  • Agribusiness

    based on its reliance on government support, as well as the requirement for detailed data collection and analysis (e.g. the Bureau of Resource Economics).

  • Education

    Driven in particular by the increasing digitisation of educational content and the move to distance learning, and the associated need for strong authentication technologies.

Our deal flow will be sourced from Australia, New Zealand, Singapore, Indonesia and Malaysia to start with.

Successful companies will receive a small round of funding in return for a small piece of equity. They will then be taken through a three month program, after which they will pitch to investors to raise funding for their business. It is likely that structured trips to both Israel and San Francisco will make up part of the program.
